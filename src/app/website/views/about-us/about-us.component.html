About FloorTech

With over two decades of excellence, FloorTech stands as a trusted leader in industrial flooring solutions across India. Since 2002, we’ve partnered with global brands like Tata, Cadbury, and Reliance to deliver high-performance epoxy, PU, and anti-corrosion flooring systems tailored to industry needs.

Our strength lies in combining innovation, technical expertise, and on-time delivery. From factories and warehouses to pharma clean rooms, our solutions are engineered for durability and safety.

Our Mission

At FloorTech, we specialize in delivering high-performance industrial flooring solutions tailored for demanding environments. With over 20 years of experience, we’ve built a reputation for quality, reliability, and innovation. From epoxy and PU floors to anti-corrosion coatings, our systems are engineered to withstand wear, chemicals, and heavy use — trusted by industry leaders across India.

Why Choose Us?

  • We bring over 20 years of experience delivering top-quality industrial flooring across India.
  • Our projects span more than 20 Indian states, serving various sectors and factory environments.
  • We use only certified materials with advanced application methods for lasting and safe results.
